Our Story

Trendy Breakfast Spot in Lake Orion

The Bread & Yolk was founded on a promise to bring a wide selection of premium breakfast & lunch dishes that always excell in taste, freshness, and quality. Shortly after opening our doors in Spring 2018, we got great support from our amazing local community and customers from the surrounding areas. We are forever dedicated to continue offering an amazing experience at The Bread & Yolk!
